ABOUT THE MINISTRY

Glorifying Jesus Through Music

Ali Welekhasia is a Kenyan Gospel minister, songwriter and worship leader passionate about proclaiming Jesus Christ through music.

His ministry combines heartfelt worship, African contemporary sounds and biblical truth, encouraging people to know Christ, grow in faith and experience God's transforming love.

Mission

To glorify Jesus Christ through gospel music that inspires faith, transforms lives, spreads the message of salvation and leads people into a personal relationship with Him.

Vision

"That all may know Him who saves."
